Our Vision

To become the best musical instruments while focusing on quality service.

History

Early beginnings

Established in 2010, Musical Instrument aims to be a shop that provides a large range of famous musical instrument brands around the world. Our first store opened in South Tangerang, Indonesia.

Our founders.

Musical Instrument Store is founded by BMG Company. We love music, it runs what we do in every day of our lives, with our mission to raise people's love in music, and turn it into our lifestyle.

Global expansion.

In 2015, BMG Company announced a global expansion plan for Musical Instrument Store, with first stores opening in Singapore, and Malaysia. As part of its global expansion plan, Musical Instrument Store also launched a new website, social platform and business model initiatve to help drive our love of music globally.
